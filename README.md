# WEDPOE
Hittie Gloss & Glow Studio was established in 2022. It began as a small studio focusing exclusively on nail care, offering professional manicures and pedicures. Over time, the studio expanded its services to meet growing client demand and now operates as a full-service beauty destination. Today it provides a wide range of treatments including nails, lashes, and makeup, while also selling premium beauty products. This growth reflects the studio commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction.
